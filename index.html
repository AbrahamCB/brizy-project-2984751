What will you learn?

Mindset Shift

You will learn the concept of growth mindset and how your psychology might be holding you back from applying and landing your dream job.

Laying Foundation

You’ll set up for a successful job search — including assessing your work experience, strengths and weaknesses and criteria for your ideal role and company.

Professional Brand

You’ll revamp your professional brand, including your resume and LinkedIn profile—ensuring that it encompasses your experience, expertise, network and personality.

Proactive Interviewing

You’ll hone your interview skills — learning how to structure a succinct and compelling answer, ask meaningful questions and make a lasting impression.

Strategic Networking

You’ll learn how to apply strategically, build an army of individuals who work on your behalf to find your dream job, grow your network and

Negotiating Offers

You’ve made it to the final step of the job search journey! We’ll cover key considerations and best practices for you to get the best terms possible.
