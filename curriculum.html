How to find your Dream Job in 6 weeks (or less)

Our Curriculum

Our Dream Job Workshop shows you step-by-step how to find a job you LOVE — and get paid what you deserve. No more sending your resume out 100x a day, just hoping something will happen.

This is not an online course you buy and never complete. This is a workshop that requires active participation, live classes, projects to complete each week and a cohort of people like you looking for their dream job who will challenge you and keep you accountable.


This workshop does not promise any quick fixes, it requires hard work and we are only looking for individuals who are ready to level up and put in the work required. The tools you will learn are things you can continue to apply to your career and personal life.
